Data Mining

This assignment was part of Computer Science Seminar (Data Mining) course at UPM where we worked as a group of 4 to analyse a given dataset and generate business goals from the given data set using the tool SPSS Modeller for data preparation and analysis. The data set consisted of 99,276 ratings (1-5) from 942 users on 1681 movies collected through MovieLens web site. The analysis and the business goals are provided in the attached slides.
